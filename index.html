About Me

Safety Data,
Engineered into Insight.

I'm a SHES Data Analyst and Power BI Developer embedded in one of the world's largest energy megaprojects — the CTJV NFE LNG expansion in Qatar. I transform raw safety, health, and environmental records into interactive dashboards that drive real decisions on site.

With 10 years of experience in SHES reporting and 3 years building production Power BI solutions, I bridge the gap between field data and executive visibility — turning incident registers, hygiene surveys, and inspection logs into clear, actionable intelligence.

My tools include the full Microsoft Power Platform, Python for data pipelines, SharePoint for collaboration architecture, and DAX for complex analytical models.
